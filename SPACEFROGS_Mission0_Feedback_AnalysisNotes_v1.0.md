# SPACEFROGS™ MISSION 0 — FEEDBACK ANALYSIS NOTES v1.0

## What to look for after 5 tests

### Green Signal
Mission 0 is working if:
- Rules clarity average is 4+
- Pacing average is 4+
- Machine threat average is 3–4
- Frog identity average is 4+
- Most players say they would play another mission

### Warning Signal
Patch the mission if:
- Rules clarity average is 3 or lower
- More than 2 testers stop before finish
- Players forget the Relay objective
- Watcher Drones feel harmless
- Alert reaches 6 too often before players understand why

### Red Signal
Do not expand yet if:
- Players do not understand turn structure
- Players cannot explain Drone AI back to you
- Tech Specialist feels useless
- Extraction feels boring
- The game feels generic

## First balancing questions

If too easy:
- Add Pursuit Hound at Alert 4
- Drones aggressive at Alert 2
- Relay requires 4 progress

If too hard:
- Use only 2 Watcher Drones
- Hound removed
- Alert 6 gives one warning round before loss

If too slow:
- Reduce Relay to 2 Decode Progress
- Move relay closer
- Reduce cover count

If too chaotic:
- Remove optional Hound
- Remove cover reroll edge cases
- Keep Noise rules simple

Ask testers:
