# SPACEFROGS™ QUICKSTART MISSION 0 — FIRST CONTACT
## Playtest Sheet v1.0

STATUS: First public playtest draft
MODE: Co-op PvE
PLAYERS: 1–4
ESTIMATED TIME: 20–35 minutes
GOAL: Test the core loop before adding more rules, art and campaign depth.

---

## 1. Mission Brief

A small SpaceFrogs fireteam reaches the swamp edge where a corrupted signal relay is pulsing under the mud.

MachineUnits09 have already detected movement.

The Frogs must reach the relay, decode it, survive the first machine response and extract before the battlefield locks down.

WAKE THE FROG. BREAK THE MACHINE.

---

## 2. What This Mission Tests

Use this mission to answer:

1. Do players understand movement quickly?
2. Do players understand range and line of sight?
3. Does the relay objective create pressure?
4. Do Watcher Drones feel readable but dangerous?
5. Does the mission end with a cinematic moment?
6. Does the game feel like SPACEFROGS, not generic sci-fi?

---

## 3. Components Needed

### Frog Units
Use 3 Frog units:
- Leader
- Ranger
- Tech Specialist

### Enemy Units
Use:
- 3 Watcher Drones

Optional if players learn quickly:
- 1 Pursuit Hound

### Board
Use a small modular battlefield:
- Approx. 12×12 zones, squares or equivalent starter layout
- Place terrain so there are 3–5 meaningful cover positions
- Place the Relay Objective near the center

### Tokens
- 1 Relay Objective token
- 3 Decode Progress tokens
- 1 Extraction marker
- 3–4 Noise / Alert tokens

---

## 4. Win and Lose Conditions

### Frog Victory
The Frogs win if:
- The relay reaches 3 Decode Progress
- At least 2 Frog units extract safely

### Machine Victory
MachineUnits09 win if:
- 2 or more Frog units are downed before extraction
- The Alert Track reaches 6 before the relay is decoded
- All Frogs fail to extract after the relay is decoded

---

## 5. Basic Mission Rules

### Movement
Each Frog may move once per activation.

Keep it simple for first test:
- Normal movement: 1 movement action
- Dash: extra movement, but gain 1 Noise token

### Actions
Each Frog may perform 2 actions on its turn.

Possible actions:
- Move
- Shoot
- Decode Relay
- Interact
- Take Cover
- Revive adjacent downed Frog
- Dash

### Decode Relay
A Frog adjacent to the Relay Objective may spend 1 action to decode.

If the Frog is the Tech Specialist:
- Add 1 Decode Progress automatically.

If another Frog decodes:
- Add 1 Decode Progress, but also add 1 Noise token.

The relay is decoded when it has 3 Decode Progress.

---

## 6. Watcher Drone AI

On each Machine turn, activate all Watcher Drones.

Each Drone follows this priority:

1. If a Frog is visible and in range: shoot.
2. If a Noise token exists: move toward nearest Noise token.
3. If no Noise token exists: move toward the Relay Objective.
4. If adjacent to the Relay: scan it and add +1 Alert.

### Drone Behavior
- Drones should feel clinical and relentless.
- They should not feel clever like humans.
- They follow signal, noise and protocol.

---

## 7. Alert Track

Start Alert at 0.

Add +1 Alert when:
- A Frog dashes
- A non-Tech Frog decodes
- A Drone scans the Relay
- A Frog is downed
- Players spend too long discussing, if you want a harder test

At Alert 3:
- Drones become aggressive: if they can see a Frog, they shoot before moving.

At Alert 5:
- Optional Pursuit Hound enters from the farthest board edge.

At Alert 6:
- Battlefield lockdown begins. The Frogs lose unless the relay is already decoded and they extract immediately.

---

## 8. Mission Flow

### Phase 01 — Deploy
Place Frogs on the swamp edge.
Place Relay Objective near center.
Place Drones near opposite side or patrolling around relay.

Test:
- Does setup feel clear?

### Phase 02 — Contact
Frogs move toward relay.
Drones patrol toward signal/noise.

Test:
- Do players understand the tactical space?

### Phase 03 — Decode
Frogs reach relay and begin decoding.
Noise and Alert begin rising.

Test:
- Does the objective create pressure?

### Phase 04 — Escalate
Alert reaches 3 or relay reaches 2 progress.
Drones become more direct.

Test:
- Does machine pressure feel alive?

### Phase 05 — Extract
Once relay is decoded, place Extraction marker at the Frog entry edge.
Frogs must get out.

Test:
- Does the mission end with tension?

## 10. Designer Notes

This mission should stay simple.

Do not add too many special rules yet.
The goal is to prove:
- Core movement
- Basic action economy
- Objective pressure
- Simple enemy AI
- Extraction tension
- SpaceFrogs identity

If this mission feels good, the system has a true signal.
