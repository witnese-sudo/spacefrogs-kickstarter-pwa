# SPACEFROGS™ MISSION 0 — ONE-PAGE QUICK RULES v1.0

## Turn Order
1. Frog Phase
2. Machine Phase
3. Alert / Objective Check

---

## Frog Phase
Each Frog takes 2 actions.

Actions:
- Move
- Shoot
- Decode Relay
- Dash
- Take Cover
- Interact
- Revive adjacent downed Frog

---

## Shooting
Roll attack dice.
Each success hits.

Starter success rule:
- 4+ = success

Target rolls Defense if needed:
- Defense value shows the roll needed to avoid damage.

Keep combat simple in first test:
- Each uncancelled hit = 1 damage.

---

## Relay Decode
A Frog adjacent to the Relay may spend 1 action to decode.

Tech Specialist:
- Adds 1 Decode Progress.
- No Noise.

Other Frog:
- Adds 1 Decode Progress.
- Add 1 Noise.

Relay needs 3 Decode Progress.

---

## Noise
Noise attracts MachineUnits09.

Add Noise when:
- Ranger uses Leap Dash
- Any Frog dashes
- Non-Tech Frog decodes
- Optional loud weapon / dramatic action

---

## Alert
Alert starts at 0.

At Alert 3:
- Watcher Drones become aggressive.

At Alert 5:
- Optional Pursuit Hound enters.

At Alert 6:
- Lockdown. Frogs must have decoded relay and extract immediately or lose.

---

## Extraction
After relay is decoded, Frogs must return to extraction edge.

Frog victory:
- Relay decoded
- At least 2 Frogs extract

Machine victory:
- 2+ Frogs downed
- Alert 6 before decode
- Frogs fail extraction

---

## Playtest Focus
Do not argue edge cases too long.
Mark confusion and keep playing.

The goal is to find what works.
