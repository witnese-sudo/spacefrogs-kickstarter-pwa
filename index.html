What is SpaceFrogs?

A premium sci-fi board game universe about living signals fighting dead systems. Players control elite Frog operators against MachineUnits09 — a cold digital threat network that hunts, adapts and tries to turn life into predictable behavior.

Frog-first operators

Amphibian commandos with compact, athletic bodies, glowing emerald visor light, wet-grip movement and role-specific silhouettes.

Living armor

Frog//Skin is a living biotech interface between frog, suit, signal, energy, biology and battlefield.

MachineUnits09

Θ9 drones, hounds, towers, nodes, constructs and bosses act like a pressure system — not just enemies on a board.

How it plays

SPACEFROGS™ blends fast tactical miniatures combat with mission objectives, role abilities, modular boards, environmental triggers and AI-driven machine opposition.

Advance / Secure

Weapons up, sectors covered, controlled stances, wall movement, entry pressure and overwatch timing.

Scan / Decode

Blips, scanner reads, protocol cards and mission timers create pressure before the machine fully reveals itself.

Hold / Extract

Complete the objective, keep the squad alive and get out before the machine closes the loop.

First Contact Core Box

The active starter-box baseline: compact, playable, expandable and designed as the first gateway into the SPACEFROGS™ universe.

Contents baseline

  • 8 miniatures: Leader, Ranger, Tech, 3 Watcher Drones and 2 Pursuit Hounds
  • 2 booklets: Quickstart // First Contact and Core Rules Reference
  • 18 cards, 56 tokens, 4 tools and 6 branded d6
  • 2 double-sided boards
  • 3-mission intro arc: First Contact, Escalation Pulse, Relay Extraction

Design promise

  • Fast to learn, tactical to master
  • Frog squad identity before generic sci-fi armor
  • Machine enemies with protocol behavior
  • Living modular terrain and expandable mission logic
  • No fake prices, no inflated claims — only product truth
